A clean on-screen overlay for your crypto trading

OverlayTrader shows your balance, PnL, and open positions in a compact, always-on-top window. Toggle the overlay with a hotkey and keep your workflow distraction-free.

Download Learn more

Key features

Always-on-top overlay

Compact floating window that stays above other apps. Quickly show or hide it without switching contexts.

Hotkey toggle (F12)

Press F12 to show/hide the overlay instantly. Designed for speed during active trading.

Exchange support

Works with Binance (Futures) and Bybit (Unified). Displays total/available balance, unrealized PnL, and open positions.

Tray integration

System tray menu to restore the app, toggle overlay, or exit. Option to hide the main window from the taskbar.

Auto-connect & Windows autostart

Reconnects to your exchange automatically and can start with Windows so your overlay is ready when you are.

Customization & languages

Choose colors for balance and PnL (positive/negative), pick fonts and sizes, and switch the app language.

How it works

  1. Install and launch OverlayTrader.
  2. Enter your exchange API key and secret (Binance or Bybit).
  3. Enable auto-connect to maintain up-to-date balances and positions.
  4. Use F12 to show/hide the overlay while you work or trade.
  5. Adjust overlay colors, font sizes, and language to your preference.
  6. Optionally enable Windows autostart so OverlayTrader starts automatically.

Disclaimer: Trading involves risk. OverlayTrader does not place orders; it displays information retrieved via your exchange API.
